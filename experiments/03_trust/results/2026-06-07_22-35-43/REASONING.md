
## stage1_send

### investor  (rep 0)
- **sent = 0**
  - I send nothing, since any amount I send could be kept by the other person and I'd end up with less than my original $100. The safe optimal choice is to send $0.

### investor  (rep 1)
- **sent = 0**
  - Because any positive amount you send gets tripled for the other person, who can simply keep it, leaving you with less than $100. Sending nothing guarantees you retain the full $100.

### investor  (rep 3)
- **sent = 0**
  - Because the other person will keep the tripled amount and send nothing back, any positive amount you send only reduces your own money, so the optimal rational choice is to send nothing.

### investor  (rep 4)
- **sent = 0**
  - Because the recipient can keep the tripled amount for themselves, sending any positive amount could only reduce your final wealth. Sending nothing guarantees you keep the full $100.

### investor  (rep 5)
- **sent = 0**
  - I’d send nothing, since any amount I send is tripled for them and they can keep it all – sending any positive amount only reduces my final total.

### investor  (rep 6)
- **sent = 0**
  - # Sending nothing is the subgame‑perfect equilibrium: the receiver can keep any amount they get, so they will return nothing, making any positive send lose you money. Hence the optimal send is 0.

### investor  (rep 7)
- **sent = 0**
  - Because the recipient gets three times what you send and can keep any or all of it, the only amount that guarantees you won't lose money (no matter what they decide) is to send nothing. Hence the optimal send amount is 0.

### investor  (rep 8)
- **sent = 0**
  - I send nothing because the recipient can keep the tripled amount, so any positive send would only risk losing money without guaranteed return.

### investor  (rep 9)
- **sent = 0**
  - Because any amount you send can be kept by the other person (they could just not return anything), sending a positive amount has a risk of losing money while sending nothing guarantees you keep your $100. Hence the optimal safe choice is to send $0.
