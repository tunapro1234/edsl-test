Note - All problems involves binary choice between a sure payoff (Medium) and a risky option with two possible outcomes (High and Low). For example, Problem 1 describes a choice between loss of NIS 21.4 for sure, and a gamble that yields a loss of NIS 8.7 with probability of 0.06 and a loss of NIS 22.8 otherwise.

The proportion of participants who chose risk in the decision under risk task, the decisions from sampling task, and decisions from feedback task is presented in the Description, Sampling, and Feedback Columns respectively.
